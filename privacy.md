# Privacy Policy — Balance
**Last updated:** May 13, 2026

**Balance** (“we,” “the app”) is developed by **Michel Santos (MS Labs)**. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app operates.

### 1. Health and Progress Data
We believe your health data is private. Therefore, the following data is stored **exclusively on your device**:
*   Weight logs, goals, and notes.
*   Progress photos.
*   Personalized settings.

We do not have access to, collect, or transmit this information to our servers.

### 2. Synchronization and Backup (Premium)
If you choose to use synchronization, your data will be stored in your personal **iCloud** account using Apple’s **CloudKit** infrastructure. We do not have access to the content stored in your iCloud. Apple’s privacy policy applies (apple.com/privacy).

### 3. Apple Health
If you authorize integration with the **Health app**, Balance can read and write weight and height data. This communication occurs locally on your iPhone, and the data follows the same privacy rules mentioned above.

### 4. Usage Analytics
To continuously improve the app's experience and design, we use the **Amplitude** SDK to collect **anonymous usage data**.
*   **What we collect:** Interface interactions (e.g., button clicks and performance metrics).
*   **What we DO NOT collect:** Your weight, photos, or any information that personally identifies you.
*   **Purpose:** Strictly for technical improvement and product usability.

### 5. Third Parties and Advertising
*   **Ad-Free:** Balance is 100% free of third-party advertising. We do not share data with ad networks.
*   **Animations:** We use the **Lottie** SDK (Airbnb) solely for visual animation rendering, with no data collection.
*   **Payments:** Subscriptions are processed via **Apple StoreKit**. We do not have access to your financial information or credit card details.

### 6. Data Deletion
To delete all data, simply uninstall the app from your device. To remove data from iCloud: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Balance → Delete Data.

# Privacy Policy — Balance

**Last updated: May 13, 2026**

Balance ("we", "app") is developed by Michel Santos.

---

## Data Collected

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Body weight entries and notes
- Height and weight goal
- Progress photos
- App preferences and settings

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of this data.

---

## iCloud Sync (Premium)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in **your private iCloud account** using Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. We have no access to it. Apple's privacy policy applies: [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy)

---

## Apple Health

If you enable Health sync, the app reads and writes body weight and height to Apple Health on your device. This data never leaves your device or your personal iCloud account.

---

## Third Parties

We use **no advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or third-party data processors.**

Subscription billing is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment information.

---

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply delete the app from your device.

To remove iCloud data: **iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Balance → Delete Data.**

---

## Children's Privacy

Balance does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no social features or user-generated content visible to others.

---

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
